About Me

I am an aspiring web developer. I am in the process of getting UC Berkeley certificaiton in full stack development. I was in the tech industry as a developer and a manager for almost 17 years before I took a break for 5+ years to spend time with my family. My experience in the past has been in networking and embedded systems programming esp. with emphasis on Layer 2 and Layer 3 protocols. My curiosity for Web Development got strong when I started thinking about building my own web sites for few ideas that I have been floating in my mind. I started off with a beginning Python class from Hackbright Academy. Having finished that I wanted to get more deeper into full stack development and hence joined UC Berkeley Bootcamp course.

Now a little bit about my educational background. I have both Bachelors and Masters in Computer Science. I graduated from Arizona State University in 1995 with Masters in Computer Science. I worked for Apple and Cisco after that.

I live in Menlo Park, CA. I have a beautiful family of three boys. My husband and my two sons. My favorite activity is traveling with my family. I enjoy learning new cultures and seeing new places. And I am a pretty good cook too. Love vegetables and last but not least love red wine.
